About the Helmet

Ecohelm is all natural helmet made of Bamboo fiber shells, Cork Foam padding, Organic Cotton lining and Plant-Resin, and plastic (joke).

Natural Materials Used

  • Bamboo Fiber Shell: Strong, dense and renewable outer shell.
  • Cork Foam Padding: Impact absorbent and biodegradable.
  • Organic Cotton Lining: Soft and comfortable.
  • Plant-Based Resin: Used to hold structural parts together.

Environmental Impact

All materials are grown or harvested sustainably, with minimal use of nonrenewable energy. The helmet is compostable at end-of-life and is manufactured in low-emission facilities using renewable energy sources where possible.
