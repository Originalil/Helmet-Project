About It

EcoHelm is a helmet made using only natural materials that are better for the environment. It’s strong, safe, and made to protect your head while also protecting the Earth. We care about how things are made and what happens to them after you’re done using them. That’s why EcoHelm is designed to be reusable, compostable, and cool-looking too!

Ingredients

  • Bamboo Fiber Shell: A tough and durable outer shell made from fast-growing bamboo plants.
  • Cork Foam Padding: A soft, spongy material that absorbs impact and is 100% natural.
  • Organic Cotton Lining: A breathable and comfy layer that touches your head.
  • Plant-Based Resin: A glue-like material made from plants to hold the helmet together.

About the Helmet

EcoHelm is an all-natural helmet made of bamboo fiber shells, cork foam padding, organic cotton lining, and resin derived from plants (and plastic – just kidding!). It’s designed to keep you safe and help save the planet at the same time.

Natural Materials Used

  • Bamboo Fiber Shell: It’s strong, dense, and a renewable outer shell.
  • Cork Foam Padding: Impact absorbent and biodegradable.
  • Organic Cotton Lining: Soft and comfortable.
  • Plant-Based Resin: Used to hold parts together.

Environmental Impact

All materials are grown or harvested. The helmet is compostable and is manufactured in low-pollution facilities using renewable energy sources where possible.

Why Choose EcoHelm?

  • NO plastic
  • Built for safety.
  • Fully recyclable and compostable.
  • Made from 100% renewable materials.
