EcoGuard Helmet

Our priority is not you, it's the helmet

Materials

Bamboo Fiber Shell

The outside of the helmet. Bamboo is super strong and grows fast. Great for keeping your head safe.

Cork Foam Padding

Inside the helmet. Cork is soft and bouncy, so it helps stop head injuries if you fall.

Organic Cotton Lining

Touches your head. It feels comfy and uses no harmful chemicals to grow.

Plant-Based Resin

Natural glue made from plants like tree sap. It holds all the parts together.

Why Choose This Helmet?

This helmet isn’t just safe — it’s smart. Made from natural stuff that helps the Earth, it still protects your head and feels comfy.
