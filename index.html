Eco Helmet

Our priority is not you, it's the helmet

Materials

Bamboo Fiber Shell

The outside part of the helmet is made from bamboo. Bamboo grows super fast, so it’s great for the environment. Even though it's a plant, it's really tough and strong — perfect for protecting your head if you fall or crash.

Cork Foam Padding

The inside padding is made from cork, which comes from trees. It’s soft, springy, and helps keep your head from getting hurt. It also helps the helmet fit nicely without being too tight or itchy.

Organic Cotton Lining

This part touches your head. It’s made from soft organic cotton, which means no bad chemicals were used to grow it. It feels nice and keeps your head from getting too sweaty.

Plant Based Resin

This is the natural glue that holds the helmet together. It’s made from things like tree sap, not plastic, so it’s safer for nature and still super strong.

Why Choose This Helmet?

Most helmets are made with a lot of plastic, which can hurt the planet when thrown away. Our EcoGuard Helmet uses materials that come from nature and break down over time, so they don’t pollute the Earth. It’s also built to protect your head just like other helmets. It’s strong, soft, and super comfy to wear. Plus, you can feel good knowing you’re helping the Earth every time you put it on.

About the EcoGuard Project

We started EcoGuard because we wanted to make the world better while keeping people safe. We thought, "What if helmets could protect your head and help the environment too?" So we looked for materials that were strong and eco-friendly. We tested different ideas, changed our designs a lot, and finally made a helmet that we’re proud of. EcoGuard is about being smart, safe, and green — all at the same time.

Design

The helmet is smooth, round, and lightweight. It comes in earthy colors like forest green and sandy brown to match its nature-based materials. Each curve was shaped to make it strong without looking bulky. It even has small air vents to keep your head cool during rides or activities.
