Eco Helmet

Our priority is not you, it's the helmet

Materials

Bamboo Fiber Shell

The outside part of the helmet is made from bamboo. Bamboo grows super fast, so it’s great for the environment. The bambo is also tough and strong, which is perfect for protecting your head if you fall or crash.

Cork Foam Padding

The inside padding is made from cork, which comes from trees. It’s soft, springy, and absorbs any impact.

Organic Cotton Lining

This part touches your head. It’s made from soft organic cotton, grown naturally, and it has a nice feel for comfort.

Plant Based Resin

This is the natural glue that holds the helmet together. It’s made from things most commonly like tree sap.

Why Choose This Helmet?

Most helmets are made with a lot of plastic, which contributes to the 8 million pieces of plastic thrown into the ocean. This Helmet is created with materials that come from nature and break down over time, so they don’t pollute the Earth.

How it helps the earth.

Our Helmet, helps preserve the earth, because instead of using materials like plastic or synthetic materials that hurt the earth we use All-natural resources like bamboo, cork, Plant Resin, and organic Cotton. It reduces the plastic in ocean by a ton!

About the EcoGuard Project

This Helmet took days of research to find the specific materials good enough for a all-natural helmet.The helmet comes in multiple colors, and was made to be a perfect fit, without looking like a cheap and bulky helmet.
